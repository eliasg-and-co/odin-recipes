Rice Dish - the best mom dish there ever was

Rice Dish is what I call it. I think it's actually referred to as Creamy Chicken and Rice but I've had it so many times that I called it my own thing. It's the recipe I would request as my first meal home from college each semester, it's the most comfort-food meal that my mom has made me, and it remains one of my favorite dishes well into adulthood.

It's one pot, so all you people that hat doing dishes can rejoice, just don't let it soak too long or that cheese is going to stick. You can substitute things, but don't skimp on the cheese or the wine, for cooking. This meal will also last you the rest of the week if you pace yourself, which is hard with something this comforting and frankly not that healthy. Enjoy, or else!!!

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup of Olive Oil
  2. 4 tbsp butter
  3. 1 medium onion, white or yellow. If you go red you're a sicko
  4. 2 large carrots, but you can use baby too
  5. 1.5 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
  6. Salt, duh
  7. Black pepper
  8. 2 bay leaves, which are optional. I don't know many 20-somethings with bay leaves in their pantries
  9. 1 cup dry white wine, but who are we kidding, keep the bottle out
  10. 5 cups chicken broth
  11. 2 cups Jasmine rice
  12. 1 garlic head
  13. 1/3 cup parsley
  14. 1/2 cup (more) parmesan cheese
